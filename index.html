About Me

Hello and welcome to my world of threads and needles! I am AFOULKI WISSAL, passionate about embroidery. Each stitch, each thread, tells a unique story, and it is this magic that I explore through my creations. Embroidery for me is much more than needlepoints of thread; it's a way of capturing emotions, moments and transforming them into something tangible and beautiful.

My goal is to share this love for embroidery by offering my creations and my inspirations Explore these pages and immerse yourself in the colorful and detailed world of embroidery.

Thank you for joining this creative adventure, and I hope you find as much joy in discovering these creations as I did in designing them!
